Charity Event Volunteer

Overview

Charity Event Volunteer tasks include:

  • Assist with setting up tables, booths, signage, and equipment.
  • Organize materials such as registration forms, donation bins, or promotional items
  • Decorating and preparing the event venue.
  • Manage check-in and registration tables
  • Answer basic questions and offer friendly support

Charity Event Volunteers are ideally available for a total of 3-4 shifts per week for any one of our Charity events.
