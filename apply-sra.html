Skating Rink Attendant

Overview

Skate Rink Attendant tasks include:

  • Supervising the rink alongside Staff to ensure visitors are skating safely
  • Assisting with skate rentals (i.e., sizing, customer service)
  • Supporting skate maintenance (i.e., inventory organization and cleaning) and rink maintenance (i.e., shoveling/clearing walkways)
  • Setting up rink area before public skating begins (i.e., moving signs, setting up skate desk)
  • Assisting with flow of rink during peak times

Skate Rink Attendants are ideally available for a total of 2-4 shifts per month for any one of our skating programs (Holiday Public Skate, Weekend Public Skate, etc.).
