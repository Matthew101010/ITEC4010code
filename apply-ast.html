After School Tutor — Math

Overview

Tutor's tasks include:

  • Identify the student’s strengths and weaknesses related to Mathematics.
  • Review school materials, assignments, math excersizesand test results.
  • Prepare structured lessons tailored to the student’s level.
  • Assist with homework, assignments, projects, and exam preparation.
  • Support students in building confidence in their abilities.
  • Create a positive and patient learning environment, as well as Encouraging questions and active participation.

After School Tutors are ideally available for 1-2 hours after school for 3 out of the 5 weekdays.
